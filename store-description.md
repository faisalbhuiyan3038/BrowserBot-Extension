# BrowserBot - AI-Powered Browser Automation

**BrowserBot** is your ultimate AI-powered browser extension for automating tab management, bookmark organization, and intelligent page interactions. Seamlessly integrate with multiple AI providers like **Ollama, OpenAI API, and Chrome's built-in AI** to supercharge your browsing experience.

### Key Features:

✅ **Auto-Group Tabs with AI** – Let AI automatically categorize and group your tabs for a clutter-free browsing experience. Works with existing tab groups and preserves your organization.

✅ **Ask About Any Page** – Get instant AI-powered insights about the current page content. Extract summaries, ask questions, and analyze content without leaving your tab.

✅ **Smart Bookmark Management** – Automatically organize and group bookmarks using AI. Create folders, move bookmarks, and maintain a clean bookmark structure effortlessly.

✅ **Multi-Tab Chat** – Chat with multiple tabs simultaneously, extracting insights and summaries without switching contexts.

✅ **DevTools Integration** – Access AI-powered debugging assistance directly in your browser's developer tools with the AI Debugger panel.

✅ **Cross-Browser Support** – Works seamlessly on Chrome, Firefox, and other Chromium-based browsers.

✅ **Multiple AI Providers** – Choose from **Ollama, OpenAI API, or Chrome's built-in AI** for flexible and powerful automation. Supports streaming responses and reasoning capabilities.

✅ **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Quickly toggle features with customizable keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Shift+B for popup, Ctrl+Shift+A for Ask Page).

✅ **Conversation History** – Save and manage your AI chat conversations for future reference.
